FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I know if my child needs remedial support?

If your child struggles with concentration, coordination, or keeping up at school, an early consultation can help identify practical next steps and support needs.

What age groups do you support?

Support is tailored to each learner's functional level. Contact us for guidance on your child's age, stage, and learning profile.

What does a consultation include?

A consultation reviews your concerns, your child's current challenges, and recommends a personalised intervention approach.

How often should my child attend sessions?

Session frequency depends on your child's needs and goals. Recommendations are made after the initial consultation and adjusted as progress develops.

Do you work with schools or teachers?

Yes. Where needed, support can align with school expectations so your child receives consistent strategies at home and in the classroom.

Can we start even if we are unsure about a diagnosis?

Absolutely. You do not need a formal diagnosis to begin support. Early guidance can still be valuable in addressing learning difficulties and developmental concerns.
